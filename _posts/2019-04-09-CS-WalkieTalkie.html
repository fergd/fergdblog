Project Snapshot

  • Role: UX Strategist (mid-project join)
  • Goal: Clarify and deliver a voice communication feature to replace radio chatter in the field.

My Approach

  • Validated earlier contextual findings showing fragmented communication workflows.
  • Rewrote and organized stories based on dispatcher needs and backend limits.
  • Mapped user/system flows, created edge-case UI states, and built a working prototype.

Outcomes

  • Feature stories unblocked: [Placeholder]

What I Learned

Designing for reliability in mission-critical workflows means building trust through clarity. The visual flow and prototype brought alignment across the board, ensuring a stable and usable feature on release.
