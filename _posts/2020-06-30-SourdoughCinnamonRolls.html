An extraordinarily tasty cinnamon roll that is sure to please everyone!

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cut into small chunks
  • 425g bread flour
  • 140g sourdough starter
  • 1Tbsp honey
  • 1cup greek yogurt (preferably homemade)
  • 3/4tsp salt
  • 1tsp baking powder
  • 1/2tsp baking soda

Filling

  • 1cup brown sugar
  • 3tbsp cinnamon
  • 1 stick unsalted butter

Icing

  • 1/2 block cream cheese
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 200g powdered sugar
