Project Snapshot

  • Role: UX Architect

  • Tools: Top Tasks, Story Mapping, SUS, User Testing, Wireframes

My Approach

  • Conducted foundational research including top tasks and contextual inquiry.
  • Mapped dispatcher workflows and created scalable IA and flow documentation.
