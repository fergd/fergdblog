Project Snapshot

  • Role: UX & Front-End Lead
  • Team: 1 PM, 1 Developer, Myself
  • Duration: ~3 months
  • Goal: Help customers use up ingredients, reduce food waste, and grow basket size and retention by integrating recipes with grocery orders.

My Approach

  • Led UX and UI from scratch for a brand-new digital feature built to enhance the existing shopping flow.
  • Created a system to recommend recipes based on past purchases and seasonal availability.
  • Worked closely with engineering to build Ajax-powered dynamic content and handle browser constraints like IE7.
