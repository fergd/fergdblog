Project Snapshot

  • Role: Lead Product Designer
  • Goal: Surface quality control data at the right time to help mortgage professionals prevent funding errors.
  • Tools: Interviews, Prototyping, User Testing, Cross-team collaboration

My Approach

  • Performed discovery interviews to map diverse user workflows across lending and title offices.
  • Integrated reports into workflows through both app and email delivery mechanisms.
  • Validated effectiveness via user testing and updated naming conventions to reflect user understanding.

Outcomes

  • Manual effort saved per loan: 10–15 minutes
  • Increased fee revenue: $15 per loan (~$375K/month)
  • New clients gained due to feature: [3+ early adopters, 40+ within 6 months]
  • User testing task success (perceived): 76.7%
  • User testing task success (actual): 100%

What I Learned

Integrating with user workflows isn’t just ideal—it’s essential. Even the best ML-powered data is only valuable when it’s delivered in the right format, at the right moment, with clear language. That made all the difference here.
